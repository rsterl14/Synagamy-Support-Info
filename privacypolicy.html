Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Important: Synagamy is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal health information on our servers.

Introduction

Synagamy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Synagamy mobile application (the "App").

Data Collection

Synagamy does not collect personal health information. All fertility predictions and calculations are performed locally on your device.

We do not:

  • Collect personal identifying information
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Use analytics or advertising services
  • Share data with third parties
  • Transmit medical data to our servers or any external services

Data Storage

Your input data and prediction results are stored locally on your device only using encrypted iOS Keychain storage. This storage:

  • Requires your explicit consent before any medical data is saved
  • Uses industry-standard iOS Keychain encryption
  • Is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties

Educational Content

Synagamy provides educational content about fertility and reproductive health. This content is hosted on GitHub's servers (raw.githubusercontent.com).

When you access educational materials:

  • Your device connects to GitHub's servers to retrieve content
  • No personal or medical data is sent with these requests
  • GitHub may log standard connection information (IP address, time) per their own privacy policy
  • We do not control or have access to GitHub's logs

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View: Access all stored data at any time through Settings > Data Management
  • Delete: Permanently delete all app data instantly
  • Revoke Consent: Withdraw consent for data storage, which immediately deletes all medical data
  • No Account Required: All data stays on your device; no account or registration needed

Security

We take the security of your medical information seriously:

  • All medical data is encrypted using iOS Keychain, Apple's most secure storage mechanism
  • Data is protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, biometric authentication)
  • Data never leaves your device
  • No cloud backups of medical data (unless you enable iCloud Keychain on your device)

Third-Party Services

Synagamy uses the following third-party service:

  • GitHub (raw.githubusercontent.com): Used to host and deliver educational content. No personal or medical data is transmitted to GitHub.

We do not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking or attribution services
  • Cloud storage for user data

Children's Privacy

Synagamy is not directed at children under 13 years of age. The app provides fertility and reproductive health information intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Medical Disclaimer

Synagamy is an educational tool only and is not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical decisions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.

Your continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • We do not retain any of your data on our servers (because we don't collect it)
  • Data persists on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data

Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, since Synagamy does not collect, store, or share personal information, these rights do not apply to our app's operation.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). However, since Synagamy does not collect or process personal data on servers, GDPR data processing obligations do not apply to our app's core functionality.
