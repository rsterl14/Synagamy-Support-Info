About Synagamy

Synagamy is an evidence-based fertility app designed to empower individuals and couples navigating fertility treatment, preservation, and conception. Created by an Embryologist and grounded in medical research, Synagamy transforms complex reproductive science into accessible, actionable guidance.

Key Features:

  • IVF Outcome Predictor - Personalized success predictions
  • Embryo Transfer Success Predictor - Pre-transfer estimates
  • Timed Intercourse Tracker - Optimize conception timing
  • Pathway Explorer - Navigate treatment options
  • Comprehensive Education Center - Expert-created content
  • Clinic Finder - Locate fertility clinics nearby
  • Privacy-First Design - All data stays on your device

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely. All your personal health data is stored locally on your device using iOS Keychain encryption. We never transmit your data to cloud servers, and we don't collect any analytics or tracking information.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection: Synagamy does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Local Storage: All fertility data you choose to save is encrypted and stored locally on your device using iOS Keychain.

No Analytics: We do not use analytics services or tracking technologies.

Location: The app does not request or access your location. The clinic finder displays fertility clinics on a map using their addresses only - your location is never accessed, stored, or transmitted.

Your Control: You can view or delete all saved data at any time through the app's Settings.
